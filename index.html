About Our School

Mission

We empower every learner to achieve academic excellence, lead with integrity, and shape a better world.

Vision

To be Cairo’s most trusted international school community—innovative, inclusive, and future-ready.

History

Founded in 1998, our school has grown from 120 students to a vibrant community of over 2,000 learners from 40+ nationalities.

Accreditations

  • Council of International Schools (CIS)
  • IB World School (PYP, MYP, DP)
  • Cambridge Assessment International Education
  • Egyptian Ministry of Education

What Makes Us Unique

  • Holistic education: academics, arts, athletics, service
  • Personalized pathways and counseling
  • Wellbeing and safeguarding at the center
